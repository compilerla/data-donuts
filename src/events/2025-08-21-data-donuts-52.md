## LADOT's Code the Curb Efforts

This month, Zackary Campos of LADOT will lead a discussion on LADOT's Code the Curb Efforts.

### About Zackary Campos

Zackary Campos is a transportation planner in the planning and policy division at the Los Angeles Department of Transportation (LADOT). His work focuses on curb management, specifically through the Code the Curb program. He is also dedicated to helping the city of Los Angeles define a standard for curb data by utilizing the Curb Data Specification (CDS) and supports the City in meeting other data reporting needs related to the Healthy Streets LA (HLA) initiative.
